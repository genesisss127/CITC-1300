Wayne Tower

Wayne Tower is the iconic skyscraper that serves as the headquarters for Wayne Enterprises. It stands as a symbol of Bruce Wayne's legacy in Gotham City, offering a blend of cutting-edge technology and corporate power. Wayne Tower is both a hub for Gotham's elite and a secret base of operations for the Dark Knight.

Arkham Asylum

Arkham Asylum is home to Gotham's most dangerous criminals, housing the city's notorious villains like The Joker, Two-Face, and Harley Quinn. The facility is infamous for its dark history, corruption, and its treatment of the mentally ill. It's a place of nightmares, and it remains a major location in Batman's crusade against crime.

Gotham City Streets

Gotham's streets are a dark maze, filled with crime and corruption. The city's landscape is constantly shrouded in darkness and fog, creating an eerie atmosphere. From dark alleys to towering skyscrapers, Gotham's streets are the battleground for Batman's fight against crime, and the home of its most dangerous criminals.

The Batcave

Hidden beneath Wayne Manor, the Batcave is Batman's secret lair. It is filled with high-tech gadgets, computers, and evidence that Batman uses to analyze crimes and track down Gotham's criminals. The Batcave serves as Batman's command center, providing him with the resources he needs to protect Gotham from the shadows.
